# Baby Corn Pro: Future Feature Roadmap

This document outlines exciting, high-impact features planned for future updates of the **Baby Corn Pro** subscription tier. These features aim to make the app the ultimate parenting companion by integrating AI, wearables, and advanced insights.

---

### 1. 🤖 AI Parenting Assistant
Integrate an AI chatbot (powered by a localized LLM or Gemini API) that parents can ask for quick, evidence-based advice.
* *Example:* "Why is my 4-month-old waking up every 2 hours?" -> The AI explains the 4-month sleep regression and offers soothing tips.

### 2. ⌚ Smartwatch Companion App (Wear OS & Apple Watch)
Parents rarely have a free hand. A smartwatch app would allow them to log a wet diaper, start a sleep timer, or log a feeding with a single tap on their wrist, syncing directly to the phone.

### 3. 🌙 Built-in Soothing Soundscapes
A built-in audio player accessible right from the Launchpad with white noise, womb sounds, heartbeat, or traditional Indian lullabies. Includes a sleep timer so it auto-turns off after 45 minutes.

### 4. 📊 AI-Powered Predictive Insights
Instead of just showing past data, predict the future. By analyzing the baby's recent sleep and feeding logs, the app could push a notification: *"Based on recent patterns, your baby might be getting sleepy in about 30 minutes."*

### 5. 🏥 Pediatrician Export Mode
A feature that allows parents to instantly generate a beautiful, 1-page PDF summary of the last 7 days (feeding amounts, sleep hours, diaper counts, and temperature logs) to hand directly to their pediatrician during checkups.

### 6. 🎙️ Voice Logging
Integrate voice-to-text so exhausted parents can just open the app and say: *"Logged a dirty diaper at 3 PM"* or *"Fed 120ml formula,"* and the app automatically parses the intent and saves the record.

### 7. 📈 WHO Growth Percentile Overlays
In the Growth Tab, overlay the World Health Organization (WHO) standard growth curves so parents can see exactly which percentile their baby falls into for height, weight, and head circumference.

### 8. 👨‍👩‍👧 Multi-Caregiver Role Permissions
For the Family Sharing feature, add specific roles. For example, a "Nanny" or "Babysitter" role that can log activities but cannot delete historical data or view the child's secure documents/photos.
